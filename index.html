Berkeley High Website Development


Web development is the work involved in developing a website for the Internet (World Wide Web). Our team uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to procure high quality organization websites.


Request a Website


The web development team at Berkeley High aims to provide BHS clubs and organizations with a resource for website design. Fill out the form below of your organization is interested in a free, student developed website.


Join the team!


Whether you're fluent in web design and want to join the club site development team, or learn HTML from scratch, there's a place in web dev for you! We believe that as programmers, it's our obligation to make the world a better place--without people to help, our code means nothing.
